>> 英文中一年十二个月份的全称及其缩写形式：

1. January - Jan.
2. February - Feb.
3. March - Mar.
4. April - Apr.
5. May - May (May没有缩写)
6. June - Jun.
7. July - Jul.
8. August - Aug.
9. September - Sep. 或 Sept.
10. October - Oct.
11. November - Nov.
12. December - Dec.
